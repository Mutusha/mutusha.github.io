His Excellency, President Edger Chagwa Lungu visited Chitambo Town Council stand at the Grand opening of the Information Center at David Livingstone's Memorial site where he was happy with investment profile for the district.

Chitambo Town Council Secretary, Mr Edger Chilanzi Mulwanda alluded to his excellence the main activities that the Local Authority has embarked on in order to attain an eficient, competitive, self-sustained and financially stable Local Authority that would ensure quality sevice delivery to the people of Chitambo by the year 2030.

The Council Secretary informed the President about the Rice Farming project that the Council was undertaking. He also spoke about the infrastructure projects that had started in the distric. He indicated that the projects were creating job opprtunities to the local communities.

Mr Mulwanda also indicated that the District Council could not be self sustained without strategic investments and he encouraged all potential investors to look into the districts investment opportunities such as: Infrastructure Development and Agriculture. Mr Mulwanda invited the President to also invest in Chitambo.

His excellence the President called on his Delegation to invest in Chitambo District. Part of his delegation was the British High Commissioner to Zambia.
