    MRS KAHEMBI  MWENYECHANYA

   DISTRICT PLANNING OFFICER 

 

FUNCTIONS OF THE PLANNING DEPARTMENT:

i) Layout plans (Industrial, Business, Residential, Marking of the road reserves)

ii) Scrutinizing building plans

iii) Field survey and inspection of construction development activities.

iv) To enhance implementation of council development plans and programmes.

v) To provide HIV/AIDS programs in the District.

The Department is mandated to facilitate a well designed and well co-ordinated land use and layout plan for the township in order to attract investment.

 

FUNCTIONS AND DUTIES

Enforcement of the public health act and other related acts to ensure compliance.

Inspection of permises monitoring of pollution levels in order to ensure adherence and compliance to the relevant environmental laws.

Prevention, mitigation and control activities in order to minimize the prevalence of communicable disease in the district.

Facilitates and undertakes the submission of new layout plans for numbering in order to enhance land identification.

Co-ordinates and undertakes effectively the design of drafting local area plans in order to enhance land identification.

Coordinates and undertakes effictively land use planning in order to achieve coordinated development and aesthetically acceptable urban environment.

Undertakes the paging out of the road reserve in order to guide road constuction.

Undertakes accurately the placement and re-establishment of property boundary beacons in order to preserve integrity of plot boundaries.

Enforces bus stops and market acts.

Produces reports to Plans and Works, HESS and DDCC standing committees for decision making.

Co-ordinates HIV/AIDS programs in the district.

Spearheads gender mainstreaming in all developmental programs.

 

PUBLIC HEALTH

The main function is to Undertakes the delivery of public health services and enforcements of laws on environmental health in order to minimize health and safety hazards

 

MAIN FUNCTIONS

  • Enforcement of the public health act and other related acts in order to ensure compliance.
  • Inspection of premises and monitoring of pollution levels in order to ensure adherence and compliance to relevant environmental laws
  • Inspection and sampling of meat and other foods in order to ensure that they are safe for public consumption
  • Scrutiny of building plans and insurance of occupation certificates in order to ensure that building developments are habitable and safe.
  • Disease prevention, mitigation and control of activities in order to minimize the prevalence of communicable diseases in the city.
  • Certification of the preservation and preparation of human remains for transportation in order to reduce risk of communicable diseases.
  • Supervise effectively human, financial and material resources in order to enhance operations.
