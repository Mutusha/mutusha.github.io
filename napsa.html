National Pension Scheme Authority (NAPSA) embarked on the construction of 50 houses in Chitambo District. This project will help reduce on the housing challenges the District is facing. Some officers are forced to reside in the neighboring district due to accommodation shortage.

The project is expected to take 9 months to be completed according to the project Manager for Provident Investment, the construction company contracted by NAPSA.

The local Authority is delighted by this project as it will steer up development in the District. The local people they have benefited from the project as it has created employment
