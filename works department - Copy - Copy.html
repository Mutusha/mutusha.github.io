WASTE MANAGEMENT

The Works Department has the responsibility of constructing and maintaining buildings, machinery and any infrastructure in the District.

The core mandate of this department is to provide services to the community by way of ensuring that infrastructure sin the district are up to standards.

The Director of Works Heads the department.

 

                               MR RONALD MPOSA

                      DIRECTOR OF WORKS

 

       

KEY FUNCTIONS OF THE WORKS DEPARTMENT

i) Buiding, Construction and maintanance of District building in order to ensure safety of building and prolong their life span. 

ii) Quantity surveying process; quantifying technical procurement support of projects in order to determine the cost of capital projects. 

iii) Production of reports to plans and works to the committee for decision making. 

iv)  Identifies the need to precure earth moving equipment and vehicles

v) Construct and maintains roads and bridges.

vii) Architectural: Coordinates quality assurance of architectural designs for private constuction and design of council infrastructure services in order to ensure adherence to architectural standards and design of district building.

vii) Coordinate all water and sanitation programs in the district and ensure intersectional cooperating and participation within the D-WASHE committees.

viii) Install Culverts

ix) Maintains vehicles for the smooth running of the Council.

x) Ensure that all works under construction are executed according to the specifications prescribed by the engineer.
