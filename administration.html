KEY FUNCTIONS OF ADMINISTRATION DEPARTMENT

 

 

Interpretation Of Condition Of Service

The Administraton Department manages timely interpretation of condition of service in order to enhance adherence and staff retention

Perfomance Management

  • Human Resource is charged with the responsibility to ensure that the employees perform to their level best . As such performance management tools are used to assess this.
  • Employee appraisal is an on going process which occurs when the supervisor formally evaluates the performance of His/Her surbodinate which is done to confirm, increase pay, promote or train the appraisal.

 

Measure Performance

In order to measure performance, the assessment is conducted twice a month.

Staff Development

Human Resource is an important component in any organization for increased productivity.

Recruitment And Placement

  • As Human Resourse we recommend potential candidates for employment to the Local Government ServiceCommission.
  • Once the Local Government Service Commission employs, it's the responsibility of the Administration to ensure the employee settles well in His/Her position.
  • The Administration Department also ensures the employee is oriented and guided on all process partaning to their new position and new environment.

Staff Welfare

In order to promote good health and well being of staff, human resource section is tasked to effectively manage the staff welfare
