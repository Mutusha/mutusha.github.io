The Administration of Chitambo Town Council is divided into two the first is a Political wing headed by the Chairperson while the Administration part is led by the Council Secretary and four directors representing four departments namely: Department of Administration, Department of Finance, Department of Works and supply, Department of Development and Town Planning

 

 

THE COUNCIL SECRETARY

The Council Secretary is the principal office and chief executive officer of the council and He or She is charged with the responsibility of overseeing the overal management and supervision of the Council  operations. This includes matters relating  to policy guidance and co-ordination, monitoring and evaluation.

FINANCE DEPARTMENT

The Department is mandated to ensure that there is efficient management of Council assets on behalf of  Chitambo Town Council  in order to maximise return from use of the assets to sustain the provision of services. The overall responsibility is to source and collect revenue; prepare books of account in terms of management accounts and audited financial statements; prepare Council budgets and undertake performance management through budgetary control to ascertain value for money; administer the employee pay-roll and carry out an update of the valuation roll.

WORKS AND SUPPLY DEPARTMENT

The Works Department has the responsibility of constructing and maintaining buildings, machinery and any infrastructure in the District.

The core mandate of this department is to provide services to the community by way of ensuring that infrastructure sin the district are up to standards.

The Director of Works Heads the department.
