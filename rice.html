Chitambo council chairperson Mr. Laston Chibuye was impressed with the status of rice when the finance, human resources and general purposes committee toured the chitambo 50ha Rice farm last week.

The chairperson spoke highly of diversification to other crops which could be grown in the district.

During the same tour, the council Secretary Mr. Edgar C Mulwanda also echoed on the need to diversify and take advantage of the favorable climate God had given Chitambo Town. He also charged that the district has potential to develop very fast if the agricultural sector was to be exploited as this would help to cushion financial pressures the local Authority was facing due to lack of revenue sources.

During the same tour, the District Agriculture Office representative Mrs. Daka said that the Rice was at grain filling stage and that it was growing well.
