Undertakes effectively the inspection of premises, food, water and monitoring of pollution levels in order to ensure adherence and compliance to the relevant laws.

                         

KEY FUNCTIONS

 

  • Inspection of food premises, such as restaurants, bars, etc., and ensuring that they adhere to the Public Health Act and the food and Drugs Acts.
  • Investigation of food poisoning.
  • Inspection of human remains for export.
  • Inspection of Meat and Meat products.
  • Inspection of building including markets, dwelling houses, schools, etc.
  • Investigation of public health complaints from residents.
  • Control and prevention of communicable diseases.
  • Water and quality monitoring
  • Control of public nuisances.
