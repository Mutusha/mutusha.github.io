Chitambo Town Council Community Development team led by Carolyn Muleya Community Development Officer successfully distributed 100 boxes of male and 50 female condoms in funsa, Katonga, Lulimala and Chalilo wards in chief Chitambo area. Human population increases in these areas during this time of the year due to the fishing season hence the need to provide condoms.

The response by the community was overwhelmed and they requested the council to source for more condoms as this would have impact in reducing diseases and unplanned pregnancies.
