Welcome to Chitambo Town Council Website

Chitambo Town Council was established 2013. The administration of Chitambo Town Council is divided into two the first is a political wing headed by the Chairperson while the administration part is led by the Council Secretary and four directors representing four departments namely: Department of Administration, Department of Finance, Department of Works, Department of Town Planning. Chitambo Town Council has met projects e.g buiding the council lodge and rice farm.
