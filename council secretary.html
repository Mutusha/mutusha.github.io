                       COUNCIL SECRETARY

 

       

KEY FUNCTIONS OF THE COUNCIL SECRETARY

    i) Manages institutionalisation of strategic and performance management of the Local Authority.

   ii) Oversees the initiation and implemetation of Human Resource Management and Development policies, functions and programmes.   

iii) Oversees the provision of Administrative and Logical support services.

iv) Provides advice, technical assistance and secretarial services to the council and its committees. 

v) Oversees Public Relation functions.

vi) Oversees the initiation and provision of Public services.

vii) Oversees the implementation of Financial Management and Resource Mobilization systems and strategies..

viii) Oversees the Election of Deputy Mayors and Chiefs to the Council.

ix) Conducts Statutory functions delegated to the Office under the various pieces of Legislation.

ix) Maintains vehicles for the smooth running of the Council.

x) Ensure that all works under construction are executed according to the specifications prescribed by the engineer.
