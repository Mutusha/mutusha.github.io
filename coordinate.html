The council secretary Mr Edgar C Mulwanda called for coordination among the government department in the district to maximize on the available resources. He also emphasized on the need to be proud when talking about the potential the district has.

He was speaking today when he chaired the district Management Meeting with all the devolved departments. He also echoed much on advertising the District out in order to attract investment which will in turn help grow the economy.

The meeting was attended by the Head of Government Departments in the district.
