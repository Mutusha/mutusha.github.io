CHITAMBO DISTRICT COMMISIONER MR JELIATE NTEMBWA AND CHITAMBO COUNCIL CHAIRPERSON MR LASTONE CHIBUYE visited maize fields where the crops were destroyed by elephants. Five villages in Chinduka section near kasanka Game Park in chief chitambo chiefdom were reported to be affected. The duo assured the famers through there representative Rev. J Mwape that measures would be put in place to ensure this occurrence comes to an end, below pictures showing the effected fields.
