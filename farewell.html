District Commissioner Mr. Jelite Ntembwa has thanked Chitambo District councilors for the works done during their term of Service which coming to an end on 14th may 2021.

He was speaking this last week immediately after the last special full council meeting chaired by Council Chairperson Mr. Laston Chibuye at Kings Harbor Pressure Resort. He added that their effort and contribution cannot go unnoticed.

In the same meeting His Royal Highness Chief Chitambo Appreciated the efforts the civic leaders exhibited during their term in the District.

The meeting was attended by the council Secretary Mr. Edgar C Mulwanda and heads of Government Departments in the District.
